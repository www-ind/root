Dr. Anand Rana, MDS (Conservative Dentistry & Endodontics), is a highly accomplished Root Canal Specialist dedicated to delivering advanced, ethical, and patient-centered dental care with precision and compassion.

Dr. Rana secured an outstanding All India Rank (AIR) 83 in the NEET MDS examination on his first attempt, reflecting his exceptional academic excellence and commitment to the field of dentistry. He subsequently earned his Master of Dental Surgery (MDS) in Conservative Dentistry & Endodontics from the prestigious Gujarat University, graduating with a Gold Medal for his outstanding academic performance.

A firm believer in lifelong learning and scientific excellence, Dr. Rana has actively contributed to the academic advancement of Endodontics. He was awarded First Prize in Paper Presentation at an All India Endodontic Conference, recognizing his outstanding research and presentation skills. He also participated in the ROOTS International Endodontic Conference, Chennai (2014), where he gained exposure to the latest global innovations and advancements in endodontic treatment.

Dr. Rana is a Life Member of professional dental and endodontic societies, reflecting his ongoing commitment to professional development and adherence to the highest standards of endodontic practice.

With extensive clinical expertise, Dr. Rana specializes in single-visit root canal treatment, microscopic endodontics, retreatment of failed root canals, management of complex endodontic cases, restorative dentistry, and aesthetic dental care. By combining modern technology with evidence-based techniques, he consistently delivers precise, comfortable, and long-lasting treatment outcomes.

Known for his gentle approach, meticulous attention to detail, and dedication to preserving natural teeth, Dr. Rana believes in transparent communication, personalized treatment planning, and providing every patient with the highest quality of care. His mission is to help patients achieve healthy, confident smiles through clinical excellence and compassionate service.
